# Privacy Policy for Access2Education

**Last Updated: April 14, 2026**

Welcome to Access2Education. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Profile Information**: Name, email address, and role (student/teacher).
- **Academic Data**: Learning styles, test results, and progress tracking.
- **Usage Data**: Interactions with the platform, including chatbot queries.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide personalized learning recommendations.
- To analyze learning patterns using our ML ensemble models.
- To facilitate communication between students and educational resources.

## 3. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures, including JWT authentication and password hashing (bcrypt), to protect your account.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use DeepSeek AI for our chatbot services. Your interaction data may be processed to generate educational responses.

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to access, update, or delete your account information at any time via the profile settings.

## 6. Contact Us
For any privacy-related concerns, please contact our support team.
